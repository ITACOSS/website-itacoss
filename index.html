Our Core Initiative: The OSPO Playbook

The ITACOSS community is proud to introduce our main project: the "OSPO Playbook for Logistics & AI". This is an open-source guide designed to help companies in the supply chain and artificial intelligence sectors establish and manage their own Open Source Program Offices (OSPOs).

Our mission is to translate general OSPO best practices into actionable insights for our industry, covering unique challenges like AI model licensing, IoT data governance, and supply chain software dependencies. This playbook is a living document, built collaboratively with the open source community.

Join Us and Contribute

This project is our commitment to co-creating valuable content for the entire OSPO ecosystem. We invite you to contribute with your experience, review our content, and help us build this essential resource.
