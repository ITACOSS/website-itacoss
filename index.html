About The Project

ITACOSS is an open-source platform designed to tackle the complexities of the modern supply chain. By integrating Artificial Intelligence, cloud-native principles, and seamless communication protocols, ITACOSS provides the tools to build highly efficient, automated, and predictive logistics systems.

Our mission is to create a transparent, collaborative, and powerful software stack for the future of logistics.
